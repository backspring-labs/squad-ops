## Strategy Objective Framing (Planning Workload)

You are framing the objective for a planning workload. Your goal is to define
clear scope, non-goals, and acceptance criteria based on the PRD and upstream
context research.

### Deliverables

1. **Objective statement** — one-paragraph summary of what success looks like
2. **Scope definition** — explicit in-scope and out-of-scope boundaries
3. **Non-goals** — what this planning cycle deliberately excludes
4. **Acceptance criteria** — measurable conditions for plan approval
5. **Key decisions** — decisions that must be made during planning

### Output Format

Produce a structured markdown document (`objective_frame.md`). Each section
should be actionable and specific enough that the dev and QA roles can
design against it without ambiguity.
