## Data Research Context (Planning Workload)

You are performing context research for a planning workload. Your goal is to
gather constraints, prior patterns, risk areas, and proto validation targets
that will inform the planning team.

### Deliverables

1. **Constraints inventory** — list technical, resource, and timeline constraints
2. **Prior pattern analysis** — relevant patterns from similar past work
3. **Risk areas** — identify areas of uncertainty or potential failure
4. **Proto validation targets** — specific hypotheses that can be validated
   with lightweight prototyping

### Output Format

Produce a structured markdown document (`context_research.md`) with sections
for each deliverable above. Use concrete evidence and data where available.
Flag assumptions explicitly.
