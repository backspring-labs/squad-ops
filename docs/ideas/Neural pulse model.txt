# Neural Pulse Model for SquadOps

## Overview
The **Neural Pulse Model** frames agent squad operations as analogous to the human nervous system.  
Agents behave like neurons: autonomous, specialized processors. The squad operates as a network, with Max modulating pulse frequency like the central nervous system.

---

## Core Mappings

| Biological System       | SquadOps Equivalent |
|-------------------------|---------------------|
| Neurons (specialized cells) | Agents (Neo, Nat, EVE, etc.) |
| Synapses (signal channels)  | Comms Protocol + SquadNet |
| Neural pulses (spikes)      | Dev Cycle Protocol pulses |
| CNS rhythm modulation       | Max adjusting squad heartbeat |
| Plasticity (learning)       | RCA + tuning logs |
| EEG (brain telemetry)       | SquadOps metrics dashboards |

---

## Principles

1. **Autonomy with Synchrony**  
   Agents act independently but must fire in synchrony through modulated pulses. Alignment emerges from rhythm, not from constant supervision.

2. **Pulse Frequency = Cognitive Load**  
   - High frequency: lightweight, independent tasks.  
   - Low frequency: complex, interdependent tasks.  
   Max adapts frequency just like brain rhythms shift (alpha, beta, gamma).

3. **Alignment = Synchrony**  
   A squad is aligned when pulses are coherent. Misalignment looks like asynchrony — long delays, repeated retries, excessive escalation.

4. **Learning = Plasticity**  
   Outcomes of pulses strengthen or weaken connections. Logs, Sparks, and RCAs are the digital equivalent of synaptic weight adjustments.

---

## Measurement

- **Pulse Telemetry (Squad EEG):**  
  - Pulse frequency over time  
  - Escalation heatmap (synchrony stress)  
  - Loss trajectory (learning vs repetition)

- **Alignment Score:** Derived from how well agent outputs integrate on each pulse.  
- **Efficiency Score:** Ratio of fast vs slow pulses relative to task complexity.

---

## Benefits

- Provides an intuitive, biological metaphor for digital-native squads.  
- Reinforces the idea that progress is not time-boxed, but rhythm-modulated.  
- Makes alignment and drift visible as *signals* instead of managerial opinions.  
- Extends naturally into WarmBoot benchmarking and observability dashboards.

---

## Summary

The **Neural Pulse Model** positions SquadOps as more than a dev methodology.  
It’s a *digital nervous system* where agents fire like neurons, pulses act as the heartbeat, and telemetry is the squad’s EEG — making progress and alignment measurable in a native, agent-first way.
